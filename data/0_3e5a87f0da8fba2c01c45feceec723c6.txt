Design flaw causes screen to crack. BEWARE
Great little piece of kit, except one problem: Your screen will crack, rendering the touch useless.The Yoga has a design flaw which causes the screen to crack after a few months. You can read about this problem online. The problem is the flimsy plastic case bends slightly when you close it. After a few times, you'll see a crack develop in the fragile screen.Once the screen in cracked it's useless as a touchscreen. You'll need to disable it otherwise you'll get random clicks.Stay far, far away from this.
