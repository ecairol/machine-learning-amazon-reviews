The positive side of this Chromebook is the fact that it has everything you need for writing, emailing, storing things, and so forth; the negative side is that, printing is subordinated to a real laptop, that is, a Windows or Mac based laptop. You cannot print unless your real computer is on, at least I have not figured out how to connect my new cloud ready printer to it directly, if there is such thing.
