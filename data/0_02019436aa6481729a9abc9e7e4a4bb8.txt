I bought this laptop to be use at the office, so I am only running web browsers and Microsoft Office. I went into this purchase with low expectations given the price, and after a couple of weeks I regret not spending a few extra bucks. The RAM can't be upgraded which is a bummer because it could really use a boost in that department.
