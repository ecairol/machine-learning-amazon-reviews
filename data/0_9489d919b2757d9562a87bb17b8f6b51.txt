Great things about this laptop
Great things about this laptop:It's sleek design, thin, and extremely light weight. The keys light up and it looks very slick at night. Also, it comes with pre installed cameraHorrible things about this laptop:1 month into using it - issues with keyboard and keys constantly sticking. Google this and you will find tons of people with similar problems.The touchpad CONSTANTLY does things that I do not want. I have no idea who designed this touchpad, but it is extremely frustrating to use.Finally, the charging feature of this laptop is via an extremely think stick. It broke literally 13 months after I got the laptop. now I can't charge the damn thing and will need to have it fixed.I really regret buying this laptop. I bought it for all the reasons listed in the advantages section, but this thing has been more of a headache then actual useful device.
